History

Pankration is one of the oldest mixed martial on record. You can derive this martial art all the way back to ancient Greece, during the second millenium BCE. Pankration is a mix of boxing and wresstling, and full contact. We see it as a sport; however, back then it was also a fighting system that the armies used as part of their arsenal. They introduced the martial art during the 33rd Olympic Games and very quickly became a crowd favourite.
