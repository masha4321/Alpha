What is Tethrippon



Tethrippon is one kind of the chariot races. The chariot races had their origin in the military customs of the Achaeans. The following is a list of chariot races in the chronological order in which they appeared in Olympia:
>The tethrippon, a four-horse chariot in 680 BC;
>the apene, a chariot pulled by two mules in 500 BC;
>the synoris, a chariot pulled by two horses in 408 BC;
>the tethrippon for foals in 384 BC and the synoris for foals in 268 BC.
